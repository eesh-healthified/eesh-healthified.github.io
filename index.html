About Us

We believe hospitals
deserve better food

EESH is a fresh, nutrition-labelled health kiosk born in Kozhikode — designed to bring genuine nourishment to patients, visitors, and hospital staff who deserve more than vending machines.

🎯 Our Vision
A hospital where healing begins with what you eat
To become the most trusted in-hospital nutrition partner across Kerala — where every patient, attendant, and healthcare worker has access to fresh, clean, honest food without stepping outside.
🌱 Our Mission
Zero added sugar. Zero compromise. Every single day.
To serve 100% natural, nutrition-labelled beverages, bowls, and snacks inside hospital premises — helping patients on restricted diets, supporting attendants through long hours, and giving healthcare teams something genuinely healthy to reach for.
